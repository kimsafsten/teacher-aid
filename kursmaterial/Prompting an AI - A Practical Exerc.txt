**Prompting an AI - A Practical Exercise**

As a pedagogical assistant to Anna Lindqvist at Yrkesakademin, I'm excited to create a new exercise that helps you understand how to effectively prompt an AI. In this 15-minute activity, you'll learn the basics of crafting effective prompts and see the results for yourself.

**Objective:**

* Understand the importance of clear and concise language when prompting an AI
* Learn how to structure a prompt to elicit specific responses from an AI
* Practice creating effective prompts using a simple example

**Exercise: "What's Your Favorite Food?"**

Imagine you're planning a dinner party and want the AI to suggest some popular food options. You decide to ask it about its favorite food, but you're not sure how to phrase the question.

**Step 1: Clarify Your Question (2 minutes)**

Take a minute to think about what you really want to know from the AI. Write down your question:

"What is my favorite food?"

Notice how vague this question is? You want something more specific!

**Step 2: Refine Your Prompt (3 minutes)**

Use the following tips to refine your prompt:

* Make it clear and concise
* Specify what type of response you're looking for (e.g., "list some popular foods")
* Avoid ambiguity or unclear language

Rewrite your question based on these guidelines:

"What are 5 popular main courses that people enjoy eating?"

**Step 3: Test Your Prompt (4 minutes)**

Open a chat window or use an AI tool to test your prompt. Ask the AI the refined question and see how it responds. Take note of the response(s) you receive.

**Debriefing (4 minutes)**

Reflect on what happened:

* Did the AI provide relevant responses?
* Were there any issues with understanding or misinterpretation?
* How did refining your prompt affect the outcome?

**Tips for Future Prompting:**

1. Be specific and clear
2. Use simple language when possible
3. Consider the context and audience (e.g., cultural, dietary restrictions)
4. Test and refine your prompts to ensure effective communication

By following these steps and tips, you've successfully prompted an AI and gained a better understanding of how to effectively communicate with machines. Remember, crafting good prompts takes practice, so keep experimenting and refining your skills!
